👥 Characters of the Red Scar

The notable commanders, factions, and forces that shape the destiny of the region

🦅

Imperial Forces

Defending the Imperium

Watch Master Sanguone
Commander of Scars Watch
Deathwatch
Faction: Deathwatch Watch Fortress
Watch Master Sanguone commands the Deathwatch Watch Fortress Fleet stationed in the Red Scar region. A seasoned veteran of countless campaigns against xenos threats.
Previously served at Talasa Prime as a Watch Captain, where he spent substantial time fighting Tyranids in Ultramar. He combines Ultramarine patience and restraint with the blood-given fury of his chapter, making him an effective leader against overwhelming odds.
Commander Dante
Chapter Master of Blood Angels
Blood Angels
Faction: Blood Angels Chapter
Legendary commander of the Blood Angels Space Marine Chapter and defender of Baal. Following the devastating Siege of Baal, Dante recognized the ongoing Tyranid threat in the Red Scar region.
As the most senior living member of the Blood Angels, Dante recommended the establishment of the Deathwatch Watch Fortress to maintain regional stability and protect Baal from further xenos incursions.
Lord Roboute Guilliman
Lord Commander of the Imperium
Ultramarines
Faction: Imperium of Mankind
The Primarch Roboute Guilliman, returned from the long sleep, now guides the Imperium in its darkest hour. His strategic vision extends across the Imperium Nihilus and beyond.
Guilliman jointly recommended the creation of the Scars Watch Fortress with Commander Dante to ensure the Red Scar region remains a stable anchor point in an increasingly chaotic galaxy.

Chaos Forces

Spreading Corruption and Disease

The Apostles of Contagion
Warband of Nurgle
Chaos
Faction: Chaos - Servants of Nurgle
The Apostles of Contagion, also known as the Pestilent Brethren, represent a grave threat to the Red Scar region. Ruled by a caste of biologians dedicated to Nurgle's vision of "blessing."
These twisted servants of the Plague God conduct horrific biological experiments, dipping victims into transformative pools to "free them from sadness and pain." They have transformed Phlegmus into a stronghold of disease and established secondary laboratories on Weepmire and other worlds.
The Biologian Collective
Masters of Plague
Chaos
Faction: Apostles of Contagion Leadership
A caste of brilliant but utterly corrupted scientists who command the plague weapons and biotechnology of the Apostles of Contagion. Their experiments create new and varied plagues.
These individuals have achieved a twisted immortality through their pacts with Nurgle. They move between plague worlds through the disease-saturated portals, spreading their innovations and coordinating the biological warfare campaign across the Red Scar.

Xenos Threats

Threats Beyond Humanity

Hive Fleet Leviathan
Tyranid Swarm
Xenos
Faction: Tyranid Hive Fleets
One of the largest known Tyranid hive fleets, responsible for the catastrophic invasion of the Baal system in 999.M41. Though the main fleet has been driven back, splinter fleets continue to probe the Red Scar region.
The Siege of Baal cost both sides immensely, but the Tyranid presence remains a constant threat. The ongoing splinter incursions justify the continued Deathwatch presence and serve as a reminder that the galaxy remains under constant threat from the void.

The Struggle for the Red Scar

The Red Scar Subsector has become a focal point of conflict between the Imperium of Mankind and the forces of Chaos and Xenos. The characters listed above represent the key players in this struggle—commanders, scientists, and ancient evils—each pursuing their own vision for the region's future. The outcome of their conflicts will determine whether the Red Scar remains a beacon of Imperial strength or succumbs to darkness and corruption.
